Touching The SummerSweet Moon 16 Jan 2010 creations

Tweet

This one is an up-beat, slightly jazzy tune. I’ve been sitting on Luminescent Orchestrii‘s Moon Song for a while (you can stream their original song here, or download the mp3 here), trying to do it justice with my work (not that I’m making it better, but that I’m not making it worse). This is what came out… Oh, I also used parts from:

Andre Nickatina – Summer In Florida Nuspirit Helsinki – Take It Back Tosca – Annanas Bitter:Sweet – Salty Air Mew – Small Ambulance Q-Burns Abstract Message – Touchin’ On Something
